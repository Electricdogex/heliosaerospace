Fleet & Vehicles

Fanmade spec sheets grounded in show canon: approximate performance, roles, and mission configs.

Pathfinder Lander

Heavy lunar lander optimized for cargo + crew hybrid ops. Autonomous offload and LOX/LH2 compatibility.

Δv ~ 5.2 km/s • Payload 15–20 t

Mars Ascent Vehicle (MAV)

Two-stage methane/LOX architecture with ISRU refuel capability. Designed for multiple sorties.

Crew 2–4 • Δv ~ 6.0 km/s

Orbital Transfer Tug

Reusable hydrolox tug with storable OMS pack for long loiter. Docking: IDSS.

Thrust 100 kN • Isp 450 s

History & Timeline

1990s: Early commercial partnerships; lunar surface industrialization concepts emerge.
2000s: Private investment accelerates—tug prototypes, depot studies, and lunar logistics.
2010s: Mars systems definition: NTR studies, surface architecture, and risk trades.
2020s: Operational tempo increases; mixed government–commercial campaigns.
